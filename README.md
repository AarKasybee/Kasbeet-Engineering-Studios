## Engineering Philosophy

Modern engineering fails when systems become black boxes.

Our approach is built on three principles:

1. **Model first** ‚Äì Every system should be understood mathematically and physically before it is built  
2. **Design with structure** ‚Äì Good engineering is systematic, not trial-and-error  
3. **Verify with data** ‚Äì Simulations, tests, and measurements validate every assumption  

This philosophy is reflected in both our engineering projects and our teaching.

## Education & Technical Instruction

Alongside engineering work, Kasbeet Engineering Studios provides **high-level engineering tutoring** for mathematically demanding modules across the electrical and electronic engineering curriculum.

Over four years of instruction covering more than **500 hours**, we have supported students in:

- Engineering mathematics (calculus, differential equations, linear systems)
- Control theory and feedback systems
- Physics and electromagnetics
- Electrical machines and power systems
- Electronics, embedded systems, and computational tools

Our focus is not memorization ‚Äî it is **engineering thinking**:
how to analyze systems, interpret equations, and solve unfamiliar problems.
